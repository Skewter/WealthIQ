What We Do


At WealthIQ.com, our mission is simple: to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed investment decisions. Here's how we accomplish that:


  1. Asset Comparison:
    • -We provide users with interactive line charts displaying the historical returns of various assets over the past decade.
    • -Users can visually compare the performance of equities, cryptocurrencies, precious metals, bonds, real estate, government schemes, fixed deposits, mutual funds, and more.

  2. Investment Tools:
    • -Our platform offers valuable tools such as SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and tax calculators, helping users plan their investments effectively and optimize tax efficiency.

  3. Historical Data Access:
    • -We provide free access to historical data of option chains for popular indices such as Nifty, Bank Nifty, and more.
    • -Users can download this data for backtesting purposes, enabling them to evaluate the performance of trading strategies.

  4. Educational Resources:
    • -Beyond data and tools, we offer informative articles and guides covering a wide range of investment strategies.
    • -From aggressive investing to conservative approaches, we aim to educate users about different investment philosophies.

  5. Inspiration and Guidance:
    • -We draw inspiration from renowned author and financial educator, Robert Kiyosaki, and offer insights from his bestselling book "Rich Dad Poor Dad."
    • -By promoting financial literacy and empowering individuals to take control of their finances, we aim to make a positive impact on society.

Join us on WealthIQ.com and embark on a journey towards financial independence and wealth creation. Whether you're a novice investor or seasoned trader, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
