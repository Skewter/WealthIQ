Government Scheme Information

1. Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) is a government-backed savings scheme targeted at parents of girl children. It encourages saving for the future education and marriage expenses of the girl child. This scheme offers attractive interest rates and tax benefits.

2. National Pension System (NPS)

The National Pension System (NPS) is a voluntary, long-term retirement savings scheme initiated by the Government of India. It aims to provide regular income to individuals after retirement. NPS offers various investment options and tax benefits to subscribers.

3. Public Provident Fund (PPF)

Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a popular long-term savings scheme offered by the government. It provides attractive interest rates and tax benefits. PPF accounts have a maturity period of 15 years, and subscribers can make contributions annually.

4. National Savings Certificate (NSC)

National Savings Certificate (NSC) is a fixed-income investment scheme offered by the Government of India. It provides a safe and tax-saving investment option with fixed interest rates. NSC has a maturity period of 5 or 10 years.

5. Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB)

Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB) is a government securities denominated in grams of gold. It allows investors to invest in gold without the need to hold physical gold. SGBs offer fixed interest rates and capital appreciation linked to the market price of gold.
