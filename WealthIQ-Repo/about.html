About Us


Welcome to WealthIQ.com - Your Ultimate Destination for Informed Investing!

At WealthIQ.com, we are a team of four passionate students from Government Polytechnic Pune, dedicated to empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed investment decisions. With the aim of demystifying the world of finance, we have created a comprehensive platform that provides users with valuable insights into various asset classes and investment strategies.

Our Journey

Our journey began with a shared vision to bridge the gap between financial literacy and investment opportunities. Recognizing the growing interest in investing among individuals, especially in India where more people are entering the middle class and seeking avenues for wealth creation, we set out to create a platform that would cater to the needs of both novice and seasoned investors.

What We Offer

At WealthIQ.com, users can explore a wide range of investment options, including equities, cryptocurrencies, precious metals, bonds, real estate, government schemes, fixed deposits, mutual funds, and more. Our platform provides users with interactive line charts displaying the historical returns of various assets over the past decade, enabling them to compare performance and understand risk profiles.

In addition to asset comparison, we offer valuable tools such as SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and tax calculators, empowering users to plan their investments effectively and optimize tax efficiency. Furthermore, we provide free access to historical data of option chains for popular indices such as Nifty, Bank Nifty, and more, allowing users to conduct backtesting for algorithmic trading strategies.

Our Commitment to Education

We believe that education is the cornerstone of successful investing. That's why we go beyond just providing data and tools; we strive to educate our users about different investment strategies through our informative articles and guides. From aggressive investing to conservative approaches, we cover a wide spectrum of investment philosophies to cater to diverse investor preferences.

Our Inspiration

Inspired by the wisdom of renowned author and financial educator, Robert Kiyosaki, we aim to empower individuals to take control of their financial futures. By offering insights from his bestselling book "Rich Dad Poor Dad," we encourage users to gain a deeper understanding of the principles of wealth creation and financial independence.

Join Us

We invite you to embark on this journey with us, as we strive to make a positive impact on society by empowering individuals to build wealth and secure their financial futures. Whether you're a beginner looking to dip your toes into the world of investing or an experienced investor seeking to refine your strategies, WealthIQ.com is here to guide you every step of the way.
